Why WhatsApp Notes?

Many people use WhatsApp’s built‑in “Message Yourself” feature or personal chats to save to‑dos and important documents like IDs, medical documents, prescriptions and licenses, phone numbers, URLs and more. While this approach is handy for quickly saving information, it lacks a search function. As your list grows, finding a specific file or note requires scrolling through your chat history manually. WhatsApp Notes solves this problem by indexing your messages and documents using an AI chatbot so you can instantly retrieve them with a simple question.

What is WhatsApp Notes?

WhatsApp Notes transforms the way you capture and recall information. It acts as an AI‑powered personal assistant inside your WhatsApp, letting you save notes, info, files, IDs, medical records, prescriptions, reminders and more. Everything is kept safe and private, so you can retrieve your important information on demand — even in an emergency — without switching apps.

Key Features

Quick Note Saving

Send a text message to WhatsApp Notes to save ideas, links, lists, or any information you want to recall later.

File Upload & Q&A

Attach documents, PDFs, images or other files to your chat. Ask questions about the contents and instantly get answers or summaries.

Secure & Encrypted

Protect your sensitive notes and documents with encryption. Add a password to important files — you’ll need to provide it in your chat to access the document later, ensuring only you can see it.

Tasks & Reminders

Create personal tasks and set reminders. You'll receive gentle reminder messages in WhatsApp so you never miss a deadline or appointment.

Voice & Audio Notes

Record a voice message to save information or ask questions. WhatsApp Notes transcribes and stores your audio, ready for you to retrieve on demand.

Voice Questions

Lazy to type? Just ask questions in your mother tongue using WhatsApp’s voice message facility; the AI understands your language and retrieves information quickly and effortlessly.

Medical & ID Documents

Keep your medical records, ID cards and other important documents stored safely. Retrieve them instantly by sending a message — even during emergencies. Sensitive files can be protected with a password for extra peace of mind.

Talk to Your Notes

Chat with your stored notes as if you're talking to a friend. Ask about saved content or documents and get quick, accurate responses.
